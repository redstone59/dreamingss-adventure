Created over the span of over a year, with about a month's actual work, a simplistic variety minigame collection for Dreaming, meant to be for his birthday (if it weren't for perfectionism).
Contains 7 minigames, 58 achievements, and a 2 hour single playthrough runtime, along with some secret bonus content.
